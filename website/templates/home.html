Description:

Thank you for your interest in participating in our research experiment. The study is divided into two parts: a conversation with the chatbot and a questionnaire to gather feedback on your feelings towards the chatbot. The study is a part of master's thesis investigating the performance of the price bargain negotiation chatbot. As part of the study, conversation and questionnaire data may be collected for further analysis, but only with your permission. No personal information about you will be collected.

Participation Requirements:

To participate in this study, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You must be 18 years of age or older.
  • You must have a basic understanding of the English language.
  • You must be available for approximately 15 to 20 mins to complete the study.

Participant's Rights:

  • Please understand your participation is voluntary and you have the right to at any time without giving any reasons.
  • If you decide to withdraw, any data collected up to that point will be discarded and not included in the analysis.

Confidentiality:

Any data collected during the study will be anonymized and only be used in this research.

Contact Information:

If you are not satisfied with how this study is being conducted, or if you have any concerns, complaints, or general questions about the research or your rights as a participant, please contact the Ethics Committee Computer & Information Science (CIS), University of Twente, at +31534892085, or email ethicscommittee-cis@utwente.nl.
